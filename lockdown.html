LockDown Situation

My current country of residence is Uganda. As of 20/04/2020, The entire country has been on lockdown for close to 6 weeks now. Reported cases are now 63, but no death with 46 recoveries.


In this LockDown, the President, H.E Yoweri Kaguta Museveni, has put bans on the following;

  • Social Distancing of not less than 4 meters.

  • Closure of all Schools until further notice.

  • Citizens and Immigrants to stay home. With an exception of Medical workers.

  • No movements within, into, and out of the country. No public or private vehicles are allowed to move. Except vehicles that have acquired permission from recognized authorities.
